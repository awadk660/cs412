About Thomas Edison

This website displays quotes by the famous Thomas Edison.

Thomas Edison was a famous american inventor and businessman. He was alive from from 1847-1931 and invented many devices including the phonograph, motion picture camera, and the light bulb. His inventions had widespread impact on the modern industrialized world. He was one of the first inventors to apply principles of organized science and teamwork to the process of invention, he even established the first industrial research lab.
